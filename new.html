About

Hi, I am Smriti Joshi, currently pursuing a BSc.CSIT degree at Deerwalk Institute of Technology. I completed my high school education at GEMS and my intermediate level education in science at GIHE. I am a detail-oriented and hardworking individual who is passionate about learning new things.

My academic background in science has given me a solid foundation in problem-solving and critical thinking, skills that I have been able to apply to my studies in computer science and information technology. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow my skills and knowledge in these fields.

In addition to my studies, I am involved in various extracurricular activities, including graphic design, which has allowed me to explore my creativity and apply my skills in real-world contexts. I am always eager to take on new projects and collaborate with others to achieve a common goal.

Overall, I am dedicated to pursuing excellence in my studies and my professional career, and I am excited about the opportunities that the future holds.
